on music

thelistener.

"Art is how we decorate space, music is how we decorate time." — Jean-Michel Basquiat

Music has always been very, very dear to me. It is the language of the soul. My parents had a rich influence on my music taste — I grew up listening to Bryan Adams, The Carpenters, Eric Clapton, Vengaboys, RD Burman, and Kishore Kumar.

I'm not a singer, and I once tried to learn drums — my favourite instrument — in university. Then covid struck and that's been on pause since.

However, my Spotify is a digital footprint I'm genuinely proud of. It tells the story of every phase, mood, and era of my life more honestly than I could.
