the eye

on photography

theeye.

This is a funny one. This wasn't one of those things I discovered as a child or even aspired to do. But one day, it just clicked — pun intended.

I was a fresher in college with a cheap phone and a low quality camera — a Redmi 4A. But our college blessed us with some of the best sunsets, and I would instinctively take my phone out to capture a glimpse, always knowing I'd never do justice to the reality.

But I enjoyed it a lot. It brought me a sense of joy very inexplicable and very genuine in nature. Soon after, I also started editing on VSCO and that hasn't stopped.

I've largely captured pictures with my phone and only recently was gifted a 35mm. I haven't taken this seriously — all pictures I've clicked are things I've liked witnessing with my own eyes.
